About me

I am a jewelry designer/goldsmith born in Pistoia, Tuscany, Italy. I got my formation in Florence.
I started this trade at 14 years old, carving wax and following the course that brought me to the road of jewelry.
I became a goldsmith when I finished my formation of a Porta Romana in Florence; I have been in this business for the last 15 years as a Designer, Goldsmith, and Stone setter.
I am moving to the stone-setting world to have a complete picture of this beautiful trade.
